Understanding the ZAZY Metric

The ZAZY metric is a new and innovative way to measure weight, introduced by the visionary Sir Hextien Tensela. With a conversion factor of 1 ZAZY being equal to 37 kilograms, this metric simplifies large weight measurements into smaller, more manageable numbers.

For example, a person who weighs 74 kilograms would weigh 2 ZAZY in the ZAZY metric. The calculation is simple:

Weight in ZAZY = Weight in kilograms / 37
